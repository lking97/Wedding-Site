Love Story

We met for the first time

August 2011

We met during our freshmen year at Armijo High in our physics class. We didn't notice each other much when the year started. We were brought together by our involvement in sports. She was a cheerleader and I was track & field athlete.

Our Very First Date

September 2012

After a year of frienship had passed I decided, after tons of pressure from my friends, to ask her out. After two hours of trying to convince her to it's okay to eat in front of me...weird...we had our first date on Armijo's campus at a picnic table with two slices of Evelyn's Big Italian pizza. ROMANTIC!.

We live together

December 2016

One year later after high school, we began living together. The experience helped us learn more about each other and how each of us behave behind closed doors. It was definitely one hell of a journey.

He made me an offer

October 2017

Now I wish I can tell you how magical this moment was and how it was the most beautiful thing that has ever graced this earth but I would be lying to you. It's about as plain and simple as it gets. I proposed to her in my bedroom and it was kind of out of nowhere. Still perfect to me.
